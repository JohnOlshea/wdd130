Trip Details

Half-Day Float

Class I–II  |  4 Hours  |  All Ages

Perfect for families and first-timers. Enjoy a leisurely float through scenic river canyons with gentle rapids, abundant wildlife, and breathtaking views. No experience required — our guides handle everything.

Includes: All equipment, safety briefing, snacks, and a certified guide.

$75 per person

Los Dientes del Diablo

Class III–IV  |  Full Day  |  Ages 14+

Named for the jagged rocks that line its path, this full-day run is the ultimate thrill. Tackle powerful rapids, plunge through rock gardens, and emerge victorious. For those who crave the real deal.

Includes: All equipment, lunch on the river, professional guide, and photo package.

$145 per person

Salmon River Expedition

Class III  |  3 Days  |  Ages 12+

Spend three days immersed in the wild beauty of the Salmon River. Navigate exhilarating rapids by day, camp under a canopy of stars at night. One of the most iconic multi-day rafting routes in the country.

Includes: All equipment, meals, riverside camping, two nights' accommodation, and expert guides throughout.

$395 per person
